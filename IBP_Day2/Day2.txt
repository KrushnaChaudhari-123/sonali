Flow of stocks  : 
1. Company listed their stocks to 'stock exchange'
2. Stock exchange provide stock information to broker
3. Users (Individual, Companies, organisations, FII, DII) they can buy/sell the company stocks.
4. After buying/selling the stocks, transaction information provided to stock exchange.
5. Stock exchange provide that information to company.

Note  : 
if users buy company's stocks then stock price goes up.
if users sell company's stocks then stock price goes down.
